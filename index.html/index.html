Math games have great intentions.
But too often, the math and the game
live separate lives.
At Math Arcadium,
the math IS the game.

I've taught middle and high school math and what I've found is a dearth of math games where math is intrinsically connected to gameplay. Most that exist on the web ask a math related question and the player is "rewarded" with unrelated gaming. My goal creating this website is to demonstrate pure mathematical concepts in action. Eventually, I hope to have lesson plans accompany each game. And lastly, THIS WEBSITE WILL ALWAYS BE FREE!
